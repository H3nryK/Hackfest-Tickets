About Hackfest

Hackfest is a 48-hour coding marathon where developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts come together to create innovative projects. Participants will have the opportunity to collaborate, learn, and showcase their skills in a dynamic and supportive environment.

This year's Hackfest promises to be bigger and better than ever, with exciting challenges, inspiring workshops, and valuable networking opportunities. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting out, Hackfest provides a platform to push your limits, explore new technologies, and connect with like-minded individuals.

Get ready to unleash your creativity, collaborate with talented individuals, and unlock your full potential at Hackfest 2024!
